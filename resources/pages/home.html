Our aim with Gathering the Magik is to make you, the user, have the chance to meet and make new friends through a new and innovative outlet. Users get the chance to join their peers during gameplay, whether it is an experience well had or a new venture. Our application gives an easy interface for searching, joining and even hosting games. Gathering the Magik is the tool you need to enjoy playing the games you like with others just like you.
